About Us

Many people want the same things as they get older: to stay in their own homes, to maintain independence for as long as possible and to live a long healthy and happy life. Living at home as you age requires careful consideration and planning. At Tender Care, the services we offer help you and your loved ones continue to live a happy and comfortably life.
Tender Care comprises of six (6) highly specialised medically certified professionals with more than ten (10) years experience in the health care fraternity. Four (4) Registered Nurses and two (2) Doctors saturated with a great wealth of knowledge. We use the data collected from the registration form you filled out to assess the level of care we can provide for your loved one.
